Hot Springs

You can also enjoy a relaxing bath in the hot springs at a bath house or privately if you are staying at a Ryokan. Hakone is famous in Japan for their hot spring resorts. .

Traditional Ryokan

The main appeal of going to Hakone is to stay at a ryokan. A ryokan is a type of traditional Japanese inn that features tatami-matted rooms and gives a different experience compared to the western hotels found in Tokyo.
